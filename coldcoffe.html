Cold Coffee

Ingredients Quantity
Milk 2 cups
Coffee Powder 2 1⁄2 tablespoon
Sugar 2 tablespoon
Ice 1⁄2 cup

Pour the milk into a blender, if you don’t have a blender, you can also use a shaker. Add the coffee powder, sugar, a couple ice cubes and blend them together until frothy. Pour it into glasses and serve.

*Tip: If you like it extra sweet, top it off with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and some chocolate syrup.
