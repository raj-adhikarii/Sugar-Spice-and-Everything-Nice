Avocado Toast

Ingredients Quantity
Avocado peeled and seeded 1
Chopped cilantro 2 tablespoons
Juice of lime 1/2
red pepper flakes optional salt & pepper to taste 1/2 teaspoon
Whole grain bread or bread of choice 2 slices

TToast 2 slices of whole grain in a toaster until golden and crispy. In a small bowl combine and mash the avocado, cilantro, lime, and salt + pepper to taste. Spread half of the mixture on each slice of toasted bread. Top with fried, scrambled, or poached egg if desired.
