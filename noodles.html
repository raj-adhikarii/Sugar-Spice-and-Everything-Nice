CSchezwan Noodles

Ingredients Quantity
Garlic 4 tablespoon
Ginger 4 tablespoon
Green Chillies 2 tablespoon
Olive Oil 2 tablespoon
Onion 1⁄2 cup
Peppers 1⁄2 cup
Carrot 1⁄2 cup
French Beans 1⁄2 cup
Schezwan Sauce Salt+Pepper 5 tablespoon
Water 1 1/2 cup
Sesame Oil Opitional

Take a wok, add the olive oil and wait for it to heat. Then add the finely chopped ginger, garlic and green chillies and saute until golden brown. Add all the vegetables in the wok and saute it on high flame for about 3 minutes. Put the schezwan sauce and stir the vegetables. Then add two bricks of ramen into the wok and pour water on top. Add salt and pepper to your taste and let it boil for about 5 minutes until the water dries up and achieves a thick consistency. Put off the flame and sprinkle some sesame oil to enhance the taste. Serve hot in bowls.
