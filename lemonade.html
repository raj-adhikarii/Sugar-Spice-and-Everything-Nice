Eggless Tiramisu

Ingredients Quantity
Ladyfinger biscuits 1 pack
Mascarpone cheese 10gm
Coffee 3 tablespoon
Heavy Whipped Cream 250ml
Icing Sugar 2 tablespoon
Vanilla extract
Cocoa Powder to dust 		1 teaspoon

Start by whipping the heavy cream with the vanilla and the sugar until it thickens into a soft cream-like consistency. Add the mascarpone at this point and with the mixer running on medium, allow it to whip until you achieve stiff peaks. Be sure to use both super cold heavy cream and super cold mascarpone. Then take a bowl and pour 1 cup of water and add the coffee powder into it and stir well. Dip the ladyfingers in the coffee mixture and line them in a flat layer in an 8Ã—8 baking dish. Then top with a layer of mascarpone cream, the second layer of dipped lady fingers and finally, the last of the whipped mascarpone. Sprinkle the cocoa powder on top and allow the dessert to cool for 4-6 hours. Better if you let it go overnight as the flavor really develops!
